Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can either sign in or place an order as a guest.

Do I need an account to order?

No, you can place an order as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track your orders and save your favorite restaurants.

What are your delivery hours?

Delivery hours vary by restaurant. Typically, deliveries are available from 9 AM to 11 PM.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time from the "Track Order" section on the website or app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept debit/credit cards, UPI, wallets, and cash on delivery (COD).

How do I apply a promo code?

Enter your promo code at checkout in the “Apply Coupon” section.

Can I request special instructions for my order?

Yes, you can add special instructions at checkout (e.g., "No onions" or "Extra cheese").

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us via email, phone, or through the "Contact Us" section on the website.
